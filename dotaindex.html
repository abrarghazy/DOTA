Dota 2
Look no further than Dota 2, the sequel to the legendary mod that changed the face of competitive gaming forever. Dota 2 is a game that's not for the faint of heart; it requires strategic thinking, quick reflexes, and a deep understanding of its gameplay mechanics to succeed. But for those who are up for the challenge, it's an experience like no other. 
How to Play
The objective of the game is to destroy the opposing team's Ancient, which is located in their base. To win the game, you must work together with your teammates to push into the enemy base, defeat their defenses, and destroy the Ancient. You can earn gold and experience by killing enemy creeps and heroes, which allows you to level up and purchase items to make your hero stronger. 
Tournaments
The International features the world's best Dota 2 teams and has a prize pool that is crowdfunded through in-game purchases, often reaching tens of millions of dollars.
